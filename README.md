# VLAN Basic Lab (4 PCs - Sales & HR)

## 📌 Objective
This lab demonstrates VLAN configuration on a Cisco switch to separate network traffic between different departments (Sales and HR).

---

## 🖧 Network Design

- 1 Switch (Cisco 2960)
- 4 PCs total
- VLAN 10 = SALES
- VLAN 20 = HR

---

## 🖥️ VLAN & Port Assignment

| Device | Port | VLAN | Department |
|--------|------|------|------------|
| PC1    | Fa0/1 | 10   | Sales      |
| PC2    | Fa0/2 | 10   | Sales      |
| PC3    | Fa0/3 | 20   | HR         |
| PC4    | Fa0/4 | 20   | HR         |

---
